What We Do

We are dedicated to saving the planet, 3R's at a time! Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.

  1. Reduce: Reducing the waste we produce, whether this be plastic, water or food waste. It's all equally important and on our website you can find advice and guidance on how you can implement simple waste reducing practices into your every day life!
  2. Reuse: Every little helps when it comes to the 3R's. It's as simple as re-using containers, donating unwanted clothing to charity shops or homeless shelters. Find more information on our 3R's main page!
  3. Recycle:The more simple of them all, recylce at any and every opportunity!
